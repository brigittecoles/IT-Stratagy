Overview

What is this assessment?

The IT Strategy Diagnostic is a benchmarking tool built on Gartner 2026 IT Key Metrics Data covering 15 industries with full percentile distributions (P10 through P90). It compares your client's IT financial profile against industry peers and identifies specific optimization opportunities.

📊

For the Consultant

Instant, data-backed positioning for IT strategy engagements. Replace weeks of manual benchmarking with a structured diagnostic that produces boardroom-ready deliverables.

🎯

For the Client

Clear answers to "Are we spending the right amount on IT?" with peer-calibrated insights, gap analysis, and prioritized opportunities with dollar-value estimates.

How It Works

An AI agent walks you through data collection conversationally, runs the benchmark engine, and generates a 10-sheet diagnostic report with chain-of-thought audit trail.
