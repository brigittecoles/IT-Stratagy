Context Matters
The Benchmark Is a Compass, Not a Verdict
A company at median either (a) has already completed modernization and is running lean, (b) has not yet started its transformation and is underinvesting, or (c) is genuinely efficient. Trajectory and mix (OPEX vs. CAPEX) matter far more than the point-in-time percentage. A CAPEX-heavy profile is the hallmark of a company investing — not overspending.
